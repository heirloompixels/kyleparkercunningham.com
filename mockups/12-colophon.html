The Art of
Kyle Parker Cunningham

Painting · Printmaking · Cinema

Believing in a future is not the same thing as living through a future. This is becoming all the more apparent as we move forward into each new day of this existence. Who, and what, are we supposed to be — and where are we going to head? This is the relevance of the inspiration, and the reason the work is kept.

The archive is the permanent record. It is organised here not by chronology of mood but by discipline and by year, so that the perception, the memory, and the quiet geometry of daily life have somewhere fixed to live.
