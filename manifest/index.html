Manifest — every page, and who wrote it

This site is a collaboration. Kyle Parker Cunningham made the art and wrote the words, the poems, and the lives behind them, across more than twenty years. An AI assistant — Claude — helped build the structure of the site and, on a small number of pages, wrote the prose.

This page lists every page on the site and marks, honestly, who authored its writing. Attribution follows the visible words: where Claude only transcribed Kyle's catalogue data into a page, the page is still Kyle's. Pages marked structure are generated index views that no one wrote by hand.
