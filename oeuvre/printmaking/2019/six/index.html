Oeuvre / printmaking / 2019

Six

Drypoint Intaglio Print, Hand-colored with Watercolor · 8 x 10 inches · 2019

Six round creatures on bird feet, eyes up on long stalks, hand colored in purples and blues, standing around the way a group does when nobody wants to leave first. Cousin to the Seven painting; apparently I count my crowds.

Drypoint Intaglio Print, 8 x 10 inches. Handcolored with watercolor.
