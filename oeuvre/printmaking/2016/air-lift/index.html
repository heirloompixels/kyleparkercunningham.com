Air Lift

Drypoint Intaglio Print · 5 x 7 inches · 2016

Two hot air balloons cooperate to lift one untroubled elephant. The first appearance of an airlift I kept returning to — the plate was pulled again in 2021. Some problems simply require more balloons.

5 x 7 inches. Drypoint Intaglio Print. Spring 2016.
