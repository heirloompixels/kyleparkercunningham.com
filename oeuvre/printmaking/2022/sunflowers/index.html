Sunflowers

Intaglio Drypoint Print · 2022

Sunflowers in a mug, the big head spiraling with seed, hand colored in burnt orange. Numbered into the plate one pull at a time — the open-ended series is documented on the Sunflowers page, where each numbered print gets its portrait.

Intaglio Drypoint Print.
