Alligator Juniper

Intaglio Drypoint Print · Printed area approx. 6 x 9 inches; hand-torn paper approx. 8 x 10 inches · 2020

The Nogal Canyon juniper, wind-bent and holding on. The third alligator juniper in the series — they are the great elders of this country, and I keep visiting them with a scribe point the way you visit grandparents with a camera. See also Nogal Canyon (2022).
