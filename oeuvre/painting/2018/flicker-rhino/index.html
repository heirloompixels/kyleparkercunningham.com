Flicker Rhino

Flicker Rhino by kyle parker cunningham

Airdrops in the night. This Rhino has it figured out. Friends with the Flicker feathers are affixed to a spider silk string. And upon the wind waves floats this Rhino of my dreams.

Acrylic on Found Object. 13 x 20 inches. 2018. Private Collection.
