Oeuvre / painting / 2018

Thursday

Oil on Linen · 5x7 inches · 2018

A small hooded being with two dot eyes, alone in five by seven inches of fog. Painted on a Thursday, presumably; named for the most anonymous day of the week, the one nobody writes songs about. Some paintings are cathedrals. This one is a Thursday, and proud of it.

oil on linen. 5 by 7 inches. spring 2018.
