Third in the Geometric Anatomy series. The figure folds in on itself, hands over face, while the ground fractures into pastel triangles — the geometry pressing in closer this time. Re-entry is the violent part of any return: coming back into the atmosphere of your own life, hoping the heat shield holds.

Oil on Panel (ACM), 16 by 20 inches, 2023.
