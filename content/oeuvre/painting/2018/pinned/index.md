Eight clouds, each one pinned to the sky with a nail, like specimens in a collector's drawer. In the desert you would do anything to make a cloud stay. So: pins. It works about as well as everything else we've tried.

Acrylic and Latex on Canvas. 20 by 27 inches. 2018.
