Two tall machines and a small one between them, holding hands, rolling on drawn wheels through a field of white and yellow. A family portrait from the far side of the future — whatever we become, apparently we still take the little one for a walk. One of the tender ones.

Oil, Oil Stick, Acrylic and Latex on Canvas. 33 1/2 by 31 1/2 inches. Fall 2018.
