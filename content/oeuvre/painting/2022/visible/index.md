A face caught in the instant of being seen — or of realizing it is seen. Painted fast and left raw, in a register my careful portrait work never allows: wide eyes, crimson and black, no time to compose yourself. Visibility is not always comfortable.

Acrylic, Latex and Oil on Canvas. 30 by 24 inches. Winter 2022.
