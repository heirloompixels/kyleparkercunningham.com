The crocodilians have seen an extinction or two and kept walking. Here one stands upright on the rust-red ground, grinning the grin of a survivor: not the fastest or the smartest, just impossible to finish off. I think about survival a lot — who makes it through, and what they look like on the other side. This one made it through, and seems pleased about it.

Oil, Oil Stick, Acrylic and Latex on Canvas. 24 by 30 inches. Winter 2022.
