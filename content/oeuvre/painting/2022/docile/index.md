Three monarchs lift a sledgehammer by kite strings. This is the image I reach for when I try to explain the work: a butterfly carrying a sledgehammer is no less bizarre than sending a Tesla Roadster to Mars, and the absurdity is the point — it hands you a new angle from which to look at everything else. The most delicate creatures, doing the heaviest lifting, gently.

Oil on Linen. 24 by 30 inches. Winter 2022.
