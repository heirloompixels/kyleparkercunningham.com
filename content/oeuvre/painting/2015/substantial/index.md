A wall of gray faces, all enormous eyes, watching a small figure who has turned to leave the frame. Being seen is heavy — substantial — and so is deciding to walk out from under it. Years later [Visible](/oeuvre/painting/2022/visible/) picked the same nerve from the other side.

13 by 21 inches. Oil on Linen. 2015.
