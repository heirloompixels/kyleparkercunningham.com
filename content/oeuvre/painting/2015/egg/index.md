A figure folded into itself inside a red oval — knees up, arms wrapped, the body becoming the shapes it's made of. Everything waiting to begin is egg-shaped, including us. Eight years later the same curled figure surfaced again in [Combinations and Re-Entry](/oeuvre/painting/2023/untitled-begins/); apparently some positions are permanent vocabulary.

Oil on Panel. 2015.
