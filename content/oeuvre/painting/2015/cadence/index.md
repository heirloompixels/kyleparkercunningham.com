Forty-nine orange circles in a careful grid, each one drawn around and around like a tiny enso. Cadence is what repetition becomes when you stop counting — the hand finds the rhythm and the rhythm keeps the hand. The same mark, made until it sings.

13 by 13 inches. Oil on Gesso Panel. Walnut Frame. 2015.
