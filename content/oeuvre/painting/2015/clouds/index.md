A terracotta profile face, eyes closed, gathering a crowd of small white spheres — clouds in the shape thoughts take when you finally lie down in the grass and look up. They cluster at the lips and crown like a hatch of weather. Where clouds come from, more or less.

8 by 13 inches. Oil on Linen. 2015. Private Collection.
