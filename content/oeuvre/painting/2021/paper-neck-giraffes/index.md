paper neck giraffes by kyle parker cunningham

Oil on Canvas
20x30 inches
November 2021

Paper neck giraffe. The rare breed of the future flatter than normal to use less water. These have the obligatory bonsai respirator apparatus which is oh so necessary in the futures climate.
