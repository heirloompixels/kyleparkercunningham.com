A golden, glowing rider on a dark bison, crossing the last light at the bottom of a night-blue sky. The oldest partnership on the plains, painted small and quiet. Some paintings want to be murals; this one wanted to be a secret.

8 by 13 inches. Oil on Linen. 2014. Private Collection.
