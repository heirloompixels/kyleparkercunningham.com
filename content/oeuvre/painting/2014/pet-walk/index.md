A figure in a space helmet takes a small whale for a walk on a leash, across a gold field under a deep teal sky. In some future, this is just Tuesday. The tenderness is the point: whatever world we end up in, somebody will still walk the pet.

5 by 7 inches. Oil on Handwoven Linen. 2014. Private Collection.
