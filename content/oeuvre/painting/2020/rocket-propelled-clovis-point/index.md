A figure in a white suit stands at ease, helmet ringed with ears of corn, a single bee parked on the crown, holding a rocket-propelled clovis point. Twelve thousand years of weapons technology collapsed into one absurd tool — the first point we ever knapped, strapped to the newest engine we could build. Pandemic painting, summer 2020. The [intaglio version](/oeuvre/printmaking/2022/rocket-propelled-clovis-point-intaglio/) followed in 2022.

Oil on cotton canvas, 24 by 30 inches.
