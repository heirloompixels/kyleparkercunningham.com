Alligator Juniper by kyle parker cunningham

Intaglio drypoint prints. Printed area approx 6 x 9 inches, handtorn paper approx 8 x 10 inches.

![Drypoint intaglio print of a gnarled alligator juniper tree with sprawling branches and dense, scratchy foliage, printed in black ink on handtorn cream paper against a dark background.](/oeuvre/printmaking/2020/alligator-juniper-nogal/alligator_juniper-3.jpg)

![Close-up of an alligator juniper drypoint print showing expressive crosshatched linework in the twisted trunk and angular branches, with visible plate tone and gray ink residue in the margins.](/oeuvre/printmaking/2020/alligator-juniper-nogal/alligator-juniper-5-1.jpeg)

![Full view of the alligator juniper drypoint intaglio print on white paper, displaying the complete composition of the broad, wind-shaped tree with rocks at its base and the artist's signature and edition number below.](/oeuvre/printmaking/2020/alligator-juniper-nogal/alligator-juniper-5.jpeg)
