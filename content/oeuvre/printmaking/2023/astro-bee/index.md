Astro Bee by kyle parker cunningham

The intersection of space exploration and the natural world. An astronaut's helmet becomes a hive, bees navigating the cosmos within the human desire to explore beyond our atmosphere.

![Black and white intaglio print of an astronaut's upper body and helmet seen from the front, with dozens of small bees swarming inside the round glass visor, rendered in fine etched line work on a gray-toned ground.](/oeuvre/printmaking/2023/astro-bee/IMG_1115-1.jpeg)
