Thread by kyle parker cunningham

Intaglio drypoint prints. Printed area approx 6 x 9 inches. Handtorn paper approx 8 x 10 inches. Line work describes an angular hand on the right pulling a string emerging from the left vertical edge of the print.

![Intaglio drypoint print on handtorn paper showing an angular hand on the right pulling a taut thread that extends from the left edge of the composition, with fine scratched line work on a pale gray surface.](/oeuvre/other/2022/thread/IMG_1095-1.jpeg)
