The work in this archive is for living with. Some of it hangs in galleries,
some is in the studio, some has already found its home — every work's page
notes its status where recorded, and the [oeuvre ledger](/oeuvre/) shows the
whole catalogue at a glance.

## See the work in person

**[Truth or Consequences Contemporary](https://torc.art)** — Truth or
Consequences, New Mexico. Kyle's representing gallery.

**[Sun and Dust](https://sunanddust.gallery)** — Santa Fe, New Mexico.
Also showing work.

## Buy directly

**[The shop](https://shop.intentionallyconfusing.com)** carries available

## Ask about a work

The fastest way is email: [analogkyle@gmail.com](mailto:analogkyle@gmail.com?subject=About%20a%20work)
— or use the *enquire* link on any work's page, which arrives with the title
already in the subject line. Phone works too: 575.519.4773.

Whether a piece is marked *available*, *sold*, or nothing at all, questions
are welcome — about a work's history, about commissions, about seeing
something in person on a visit to New Mexico.
