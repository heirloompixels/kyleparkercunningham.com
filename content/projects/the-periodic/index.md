The Periodic is a new newspaper publication focused on art from around the world. It is a tactile, portable archive designed for slow reading and repeated return.

Each issue will be printed in 11x17 tabloid format, 16 pages long, and published periodically. The goal is to create a durable record of artists, places, and moments that might otherwise pass quietly.

The Periodic will gather essays, images, and short features into a concise form, balancing depth with accessibility. It is a living project that will evolve with each release.
