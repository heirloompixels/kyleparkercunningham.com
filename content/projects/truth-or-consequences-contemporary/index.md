Truth or Consequences Contemporary is our gallery in downtown Truth or Consequences, New Mexico. It is framed as a refuge for the arts and a laboratory for answering what feels relevant as we move into this new world.

The gallery describes itself as a place “in the middle of nowhere,” focused on how art transmits lived emotions between people. It presents work from a wide range of local, regional, and national artists, and maintains a community etching press that makes printmaking and bookbinding equipment available to artists of the Southwest and beyond.

**Location**: 312 Main Ave, Truth or Consequences, NM 87901
