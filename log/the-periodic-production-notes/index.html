Production notes for a periodic art newspaper

I am building a small, repeatable workflow for a tabloid‑format art newspaper. The key is consistency: fixed grids, predictable image sizes, and a tight editing loop that respects the printing schedule.

The goal is to keep the publication lightweight enough to produce regularly while still honoring the work it carries. Each issue should feel intentional, not rushed.
